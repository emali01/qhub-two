Academy

Educational products will be launched and QHub holders will have access to them. Some products will be available for purchase using QHub tokens. The team will burn 50% of the QHub tokens received through the academy products. This will partially provide a deflationary mechanism. .

Foundation

We will invest in very early stage projects launched through QHub Platform for tokenization of business from the real sector of the economy: We know that the time has come to unite the real sector of the economy and blockchain technology. According to experts, by 2030 more than 15% of global GDP will be tokenized. We intend to take part in this .

Real estate Tokenization

Through the QHub platform, participants will be able to buy shares in the best real estate properties around the world. Anyone can start investing in real estate starting from $10. Participants will also be able to tokenize their own real estate properties. .
